Tomas

1200s-1300s
Orthodox Church
Ethiopia

Tomas, Abba (perhaps fl. late 13th - early 14th century A.D.), was a monk and one of the disciples of St. Täklä-Haymanot who were described as “strenuous in their contendings.”

Richard K. P. Pankhurst

Bibliography

E. A. Wallis Budge, The Life and Miracles of Takla Haymânôt (London, 1906), 134, 218.
