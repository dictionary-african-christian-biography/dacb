P&eacute;tros, *Abba*, was a disciple of *Abba* Zä-Mika'él 'Arägawi  when he founded the monastery of Däbrä Damo in the eleventh year of the reign of Emperor Gäbrä-Mäsqäl.

A. K. Irvine
